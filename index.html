Japan — Should You Add It?

The Case For
Early October in Tokyo is near-perfect — low 20s, clear skies, first hints of fall color before peak foliage crowds. 2–3 nights slots naturally after Banff (Oct 5–7) before arriving Bangkok Oct 8, wedding Oct 9. The routing YYC → NRT → BKK is a clean westward arc.
Weather (Oct 5–8 Tokyo)Ideal
Routing fit YYC → NRT → BKKNatural
Yan's Amex MR 180K coverageStrong
The Trade-offs
Bangkok shrinks from 10 to ~7 nights. Another hotel booking and flight segment to arrange. Japan rewards a full week — 3 days is a taste, not a stay. Adds ~$150–300 in award fees and one more hotel to book during already busy larch season planning.
