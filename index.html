Day plan & recommendations
Oct 1 — Arrive Calgary
Arrive YYC 17:30 MDT. Bag pickup → rideshare/taxi to Residence Inn (610 10th Ave SW, ~15 min). Check-in from 4pm. No car tonight — Sixt pickup is tomorrow 7am at YYC airport. Dinner: Model Milk or Saltlik Steakhouse on 17th Ave SW, both within easy walking distance.
Oct 2 — Drive Calgary → Jasper
7:00am: Pick up Sixt Mazda CX-5 at YYC airport (Sixt counter, conf CN961135523748). Drive Hwy 2N → Hwy 16W to Jasper (~3.5 hrs, 360 km). Drop bags at Miette Mountain Cabins ~10:30am, quick lunch in Jasper town.

Afternoon: Miette Hot Springs (5 min from cabins, 40°C pool — hottest natural springs in the Rockies) → Maligne Canyon (48m limestone slot canyon with bridge walkways) → Valley of the Five Lakes (4.4 km, turquoise glacial lakes, peak fall color). Evening: Jasper UNESCO Dark Sky Preserve — Milky Way visible after 9pm from the cabins' yard.
Miette Hot Springs: go first thing the next morning (Oct 3) before checkout — fog rising off the water, zero crowds, elk wandering the road. Worth setting the alarm.
Oct 3 — Icefields Parkway → Banff
6:30am: Pyramid Lake (elk at dawn, mirror reflections on still water) → Old Fort Point hike (3.5 km, panoramic valley views). 8:30am: Maligne Lake — Spirit Island boat tour if available (season ends ~Oct 11, book at experiencejasper.com) or Mary Schaffer Loop (3.2 km). Checkout by 11am.

Drive Icefields Parkway (Hwy 93): Athabasca Falls → Sunwapta Falls → Columbia Icefield / Athabasca Glacier (walk to the glacier toe — free) → Peyto Lake lookout → Bow Lake. Arrive Otter Hotel Banff ~5–6pm.
The Icefields Parkway is one of the world's great drives — budget the full afternoon and stop at everything. The walk to the Athabasca Glacier toe is free and surreal: blue-green ice stretching for kilometres.
Oct 4 — Moraine Lake (Confirmed)
Shuttle confirmed: INPC26-13891950B1 · 7am Lake Louise slot.

6:30am: Drive to Lake Louise shuttle hub. 7am bus → Moraine Lake. Hike Larch Valley trail (5.8 km up, +2 km to Sentinel Pass at 2,611m). Peak golden larch color in early October — electric yellow against blue-grey rock and snow. Best light before noon. Return shuttle afternoon.

Evening: Upper Hot Springs ($10 pp, 5 min from Banff townsite) to soak tired legs.
Larch Valley in early October is the single best hike in the Canadian Rockies. The larches glow electric yellow-gold against the rock face — nothing else looks like it. Go as early as possible for the morning light and empty trails.
Oct 5 — Lake Louise + Last Banff Day
7am: Lake Louise — golden morning light on the Victoria Glacier. Hike Plain of Six Glaciers (14 km return) or shorter Lake Agnes Tea House (7 km, historic log cabin). Checkout Otter Hotel by 11am.

Afternoon: Last stroll through Banff townsite — grab a coffee, browse the shops. Dinner somewhere special before the final night in Banff area.
Oct 6 — Drive YYC + Fly Bangkok
Morning: Pack up and check out of Banff area.

Drive YYC ~1.5 hrs → drop Sixt at YYC airport → Priority Pass lounge (Air Canada Maple Leaf, CSR) → flight departs 14:50 MDT on WestJet WS80 (B787-9), connecting NRT (2h 5m layover) → Bangkok arrival Oct 7 at 23:00 ICT. Confirmation KXOXGQ.

Day plan & recommendations
Oct 7 — Arrive Bangkok · Check In
Arrive BKK Suvarnabhumi 23:00 ICT (WS5930 from NRT). Immigration + taxi ~45 min → Check into Public House Bangkok (Sukhumvit Soi 31, Design Hotels member) late night. Rest up — it's a long travel day from YYC.
Public House Bangkok is a boutique design hotel on Sukhumvit Soi 31 — intimate, locally rooted, editorial aesthetic. Booked 55K Bonvoy pts · conf #88511655 · free cancel until Oct 4.
Oct 8 — Bangkok Full Day
Morning: Wat Pho (Reclining Buddha, 46m long) → Wat Arun (Temple of Dawn, across the river by ferry, ฿3) → Grand Palace & Wat Phra Kaew (Emerald Buddha — arrive before noon heat). Lunch on Khao San Rd area or riverside.

Evening: Yaowarat (Chinatown) — street food paradise: char-grilled oysters, roasted duck, mango sticky rice. Golden hour view from the Wat Arun side of the river is unmissable.
Skip the tuk-tuks outside Grand Palace (tourist trap). Use the river ferry (฿15-30) or Grab app. Wear covered shoulders & knees for temples — or buy a sarong at the gate.
Oct 9 — Morning in Bangkok + Fly Koh Samui
7am: Damnoen Saduak floating market (1.5 hrs from Bangkok by taxi/tour, best before 9am when crowds arrive) or Chatuchak Weekend Market (BTS Mo Chit, 15,000 stalls, opens 6am Sat-Sun).

Return to hotel → checkout → BKK airport → morning flight to Koh Samui (USM).

Oct 9 — Arrive Koh Samui + Wedding Island Check In
Arrive USM ~09:30 ICT. Bangkok Airways boutique lounge at Samui Airport — use on arrival. Explore the island: Big Buddha (Wat Phra Yai) → Fisherman's Village for lunch → Chaweng Beach.

Day plan & recommendations
Oct 12 — Arrive Hong Kong
Arrive HKG Terminal 2 at 16:20 HKT. Check in. October is HK's finest month — typhoon season over, skies clear, mid-20s. Evening: Victoria Harbour waterfront walk (Tsim Sha Tsui promenade) → Symphony of Lights laser show at 8pm (free, from the Kowloon side). Dinner: rooftop restaurant with harbour view.
Top pick: The Murray Hong Kong (Bonvoy Autograph Collection) — Foster+Partners heritage redesign of a 1960s government tower. Use Yun's Bonvoy pts: ~15–20K/nt. Central location, walking distance to everything.
Oct 13 — Hong Kong Full Day
Morning: Star Ferry (Central ↔ Tsim Sha Tsui, HK$3.4 — the best cheap view in Asia) → Central district & IFC galleries → Sheung Wan PMQ for design shops.

Afternoon: Victoria Peak Tram (book ahead) — 360° city + harbour view at golden hour is unmissable. Evening: Kowloon side — Temple Street Night Market or dim sum in Mong Kok.
Oct 14 — Lantau + Fly Shanghai
Early: ferry to Lantau Island — Ngong Ping 360 cable car, Tian Tan Buddha (Big Buddha), Po Lin Monastery. Return by lunchtime → HKG airport → morning flight to Shanghai Pudong (PVG). Arrive Shanghai ~early afternoon.
China tourist visa required for both — apply at the Chinese Consulate (US office). Allow 4–6 weeks. One visa covers HK entry via PVG and all mainland cities.

Day plan & recommendations
Oct 14 — Arrive Shanghai
Arrive PVG Pudong. Check in — book 2 rooms in same hotel (Yun + Yan in one, Yun's mom in adjacent). The Bund at night: walk the waterfront promenade facing the Pudong skyline — best lit from dusk onwards. Dinner in Xintiandi or Jing An.
Top pick: The Middle House (Jing An) — Neri&Hu designed, Small Luxury Hotels, the most Shinola-like property in Shanghai. ~$280/nt. Or Andaz Shanghai (Yan's Chase UR → Hyatt, ~25K pts/nt) in the Bund area.
Oct 15 — The Bund + French Concession
Morning: The Bund at dawn (empty, misty, beautiful) → Huangpu River ferry across to Pudong → Shanghai Tower observation deck (119F, 632m, 2nd tallest in world). Afternoon: French Concession — Fuxing Road, Tianzifang (art galleries, coffee, design shops in a 1930s lilong), Xintiandi for dinner.
Oct 16 — Yu Garden + Jing An + Family
Morning: Jing An Temple (Buddhist temple in the middle of the city — always interesting at 8am before tourists). Yu Garden & Bazaar (classical Chinese garden + tea house). Afternoon: Nanjing Road East pedestrian street (architecture more than shopping). Evening: Family dinner together — Yun's mom picks the restaurant.
Oct 17 — Split Day
Checkout. Yan departs for Shanxi (flight or high-speed rail ~2.5 hrs from Hongqiao station) to visit family — stays through Oct 23. Yun remains in Shanghai with family.

Day trip options from Shanghai (Oct 18–22): Zhujiajiao water town (40 min, ancient canals) · Suzhou (30 min by G-train, classical gardens UNESCO) · Zhouzhuang (Venice of the East).
Oct 22 — Depart for Athens
Both make way to PVG Pudong T2. Yan arrives from Shanxi by ~20:00. Depart airport ~22:00 for the midnight flight.
Aegean A3 1900 departs PVG at 01:10 on Oct 23 (so leave for airport the night of Oct 22). Yan: confirmed, ref AZ5QGD. Yun: self-book same flight on aegeanair.com or via Chase UR → Flying Blue (~60-80K Business).

Day plan & recommendations
Oct 23 — Arrive Athens
Land ATH 07:55 EEST after overnight flight. Check in. Rest — it's a lie-flat business class overnight, but still. Afternoon: gentle walk through Plaka neighbourhood (winding marble lanes below the Acropolis, tavernas, bookshops). Dinner: rooftop restaurant with Acropolis lit up at night — unmissable in Athens.
Top pick: Hotel Grande Bretagne (Syntagma, iconic 5-star), New Hotel Athens (Campana brothers interiors), or Hotel Electra Metropolis (rooftop pool facing the Acropolis).
Oct 24 — Acropolis + Old Town
Morning: Acropolis & Parthenon — go at opening (8am) before heat and crowds. Buy combined ticket (€30) covering Acropolis + Ancient Agora + other sites. Acropolis Museum below (modern, world-class). Afternoon: Monastiraki flea market — antiques, street food, vintage finds. Gyros lunch in a side alley.
The Acropolis in early morning light with no crowds is one of the great sights in the world. Wear good walking shoes — the marble is steep and polished smooth.
Oct 25–26 — Cape Sounion + Riviera
Oct 25 — Cape Sounion: 70 km from Athens, Temple of Poseidon perched on a sea cliff — arrive at sunset (leaves Athens ~15:00). The cliff temple at golden hour against the Aegean Sea is the single best moment in this leg.

Oct 26 — Athenian Riviera: Vouliagmeni Lake (thermal lake spring, $15 entry, best for a float and wine) or Glyfada beach (20 min from centre by tram). Easy day.
Cape Sounion: drive yourself (car rental ~€40) or take the KTEL bus from Pedion Areos park (€7, ~2 hrs). Vouliagmeni lake is a geothermal lagoon where the water stays warm year-round.
Oct 27–29 — BHI Conference (Yan) · Athens (Yun)
Yan: BHI Conference, company-covered hotel (2 nights).
Yun: solo Athens time. Options: hydrofoil day trip to Aegina (pistachio island, ancient temple, 35 min from Piraeus port) or Hydra (no cars, car-free island, stunning, 2 hrs from Piraeus). Or: deeper Athens exploration — Kerameikos (ancient cemetery with no tourists), National Archaeological Museum, Benaki Museum (Greek design artifacts, excellent gift shop).
Oct 30 — Fly Home
A3 3415: ATH 16:20 → EWR 21:20 EDT, ~10h. Yan confirmed, ref AZLZGO — Business class. Yun: book same Aegean flight (check availability) or Delta ATH→JFK (departs ~10:25, nonstop ~10 hrs). Both arrive Newark same evening if on Aegean together.

Terminal B at Newark for Aegean. Priority Pass lounge in Athens: Goldair CIP Lounge (accessible with CSR).
If Yun books A3 3415 — you land together at Newark Terminal B at 21:20. Easiest end to a 30-day trip.
