🏠 Miette Mountain Cabins
Highway 16 East, Pocahontas, AB T0E 1E0⌖ Map ↗
Oct 2 (Fri) — Drive YYC → Jasper

Oct 2 (Fri) — Jasper day
Afternoon: Miette Hot Springs (5 min from cabins, opens 8:30am) — hottest natural springs in the Rockies, 54°C source, cooled to 40°C. Then: Maligne Canyon — 48m limestone slot canyon with walkway bridges. Late afternoon: Valley of the Five Lakes (4.4km, turquoise glacial lakes, peak fall color). Evening: Jasper is a UNESCO Dark Sky Preserve — Milky Way visible after 9pm from the cabins' yard.

Oct 3 (Sat) — Morning, then Icefields Parkway to Banff
6:30am: Pyramid Lake (30 min from Miette) — elk at dawn, mirror reflection. Quick Old Fort Point hike (3.5km, panoramic valley views). 8:30am: Maligne Lake (45 min) — Spirit Island boat tour if available (season ends ~Oct 11, book at experiencejasper.com) or Mary Schaffer Loop (3.2km). Check out by 11am. Drive south on Hwy 93 (Icefields Parkway):
· Athabasca Falls · Sunwapta Falls · Columbia Icefield / Athabasca Glacier (walk to the glacier toe) · Peyto Lake lookout · Bow Lake
Arrive Otter Hotel Banff ~5–6pm.
Miette Hot Springs: first soak of the morning (opens 8:30am) — empty, mist rising, elk wandering the road. Worth setting the alarm. Spirit Island boat tour: book at experiencejasper.com · limited Oct spots. Dark skies: ask the cabins about best stargazing spots — they're in the preserve.

Moraine Lake shuttle confirmed — INPC26-13891950B1 · 7am Lake Louise slot

Japan — Should You Add It?

The Case For
Early October in Tokyo is near-perfect — low 20s, clear skies, first hints of fall color before peak foliage crowds. 2–3 nights slots naturally after Banff (Oct 5–7) before arriving Bangkok Oct 8, wedding Oct 9. The routing YYC → NRT → BKK is a clean westward arc.

The Trade-offs
Bangkok shrinks from 10 to ~7 nights. Another hotel booking and flight segment to arrange. Japan rewards a full week — 3 days is a taste, not a stay. Adds ~$150–300 in award fees and one more hotel to book during already busy larch season planning.
